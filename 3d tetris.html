What is 3D Tetris?

3D Tetris is one of the best games of all time. It is so good in fact, it is comparable to E.T. for the Atari 2600. On 2/26/2021, I convinced Alaric to live stream it. He hated it. However, I did not see the livestream that day, so I thought he forgot about it. I ended up making my own video that day of 3D Tetris. On the 27th, I was shown the livestream. I watched it entirely and edited it. It was released on my channel and Alaric loved it. It became a meme in IST that I definitely didn't force upon the class.
