What is Festivus

Festivus is a holiday that replaces all others. Festivus also happens on every day. Important Festivus is celebrated on the days of other holidays.


How is it Celebrated?

Festivus must be celebrated with the hording, sharing, burning, and drowning of Festivus trees. It is also customary to drink pine tea.

What is a Festivus Tree?

A Festivus tree is a white pine tree. White pine is the only tree that is considered Festivus. Christmas trees are not Festivus trees and must be burned.

What is Pine Tea?

Pine Tea is the best tea. It is white pine needles put into hot water. They are not suppose to be taken out. You should drink them with the tea beacuse who doesn't love crunchey tea!?
